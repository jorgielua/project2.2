Reasons Why Animals Should Be Free!!

  • Back in 2015 two zoo keeper's in Copenhagen Zoo surprised many people by shooting a healthy young giraffe,and feeding it remains to lion in the plubic, this is so disgusting.

  • According to Freedom of animals :Zoos cannot begin to provide the amount of space animals have in the wild. Tigers and lions have around 18,000 times less space in zoos than they would in the wild

  • Animals in zoo's are living in cramped positions or sick or dead on display.

  • The animals know that they are getting treated bad and start then start to act sad like elephants would sway their heads from side to side as they stand rooted to the spot. These ‘stereotypic behaviours’ are a sign of mental distress, brought on by captivity.
